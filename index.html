S1GK is designed as a full keeper system instead of only a glove line: gloves, matchwear, training kit, padded protection and accessories all share the same brand language.

Which glove should young keepers start with?

The Junior One Glove is the clean starting point for young keepers because it focuses on comfort, easy closure, training repetition and confidence.

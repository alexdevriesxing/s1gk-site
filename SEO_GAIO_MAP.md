# S1GK SEO / GAIO Expansion Map

This final build is structured to expand from a flashy launch site into an authority site for goalkeeper gear.

## Core commercial clusters
- Goalkeeper gloves
- Junior goalkeeper gloves / kids goalkeeper gloves
- Match goalkeeper gloves
- Training goalkeeper gloves
- Goalkeeper jerseys and matchwear
- Goalkeeper padded pants and base layers
- Goalkeeper accessories

## Answer-engine content blocks to add next
1. Exact glove size chart with hand measurements in cm and inches.
2. Match glove vs training glove comparison table.
3. Junior goalkeeper equipment checklist for parents.
4. Artificial turf goalkeeper gear guide.
5. Wet weather glove care and palm maintenance guide.
6. Team-order / academy-order landing page.
7. Country pages once actual distribution exists: Netherlands, Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, UK.

## Expansion pattern
Every new product should include:
- Static HTML product page
- Product JSON-LD
- Internal links to category, level and relevant guide pages
- Real materials, sizes, palm type and care information
- Original photos or video embeds
- FAQ block with genuine buyer questions

## AI visibility principle
Do not rely on hidden prompts or fake mentions. Build clear, crawlable pages with original product details, FAQs, comparisons, size guidance, images with alt text and helpful guide content.
